What to keep doing

Use the site as a filter, not the full store.

Since you do not have time to build "shop this look" pages for every outfit, the best move is to let Pinterest stay your shopping engine and let the website act as a polished doorway.

  • Feature only your actual shoppable boards in the selling section.
  • Keep inspiration-only boards visually separate from buying boards.
  • Use seasonal language because it creates urgency.
  • Send people to Pinterest with obvious calls to action.
  • Show your disclosure clearly so visitors trust the site.
